Why are Blue Whales Endangered?

Blue Whales were nearly wiped out by commercial whaling. Today, they face threats from ship strikes, noise pollution, and climate change.

How You Can Help

  • Support organizations working to protect whales, like WDC and Ocean Conservancy.
  • Reduce plastic waste to keep oceans clean.
  • Advocate for marine protected areas.
